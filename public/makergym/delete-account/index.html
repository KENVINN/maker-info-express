Delete Account

This is the public account deletion page for Maker Gym. Users can delete their account directly inside the app. If the user cannot access the app, they can request deletion by email using the support contact listed below.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open Maker Gym and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Profile or Settings.
  3. Select the delete account option.
  4. Confirm the request to permanently delete the account.

If you cannot access the app

Send a deletion request to suporte@makergym.app using the email address associated with your Maker Gym account.

Request deletion by email

What data is deleted

  • Account profile data
  • Authentication account associated with Maker Gym
  • Workout history, templates, sets, timers, reminders, and related training records
  • Body metrics and progress records
  • Uploaded profile or exercise setup media stored for the account

What may be retained for a limited period

  • Operational logs and security records when required for fraud prevention, abuse prevention, or legal compliance
  • Backup copies that may remain for a limited retention window until they are overwritten in normal infrastructure cycles

Processing time

Account deletion requests submitted through the in-app flow are processed as part of the product deletion workflow. Email-based requests are handled manually after verification of account ownership.
